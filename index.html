Established in November 2010, Poly John & Company has been a pillar of financial integrity and expert advisory for over fifteen years.

Based in Thodupuzha, Kerala, our firm specialises in comprehensive accounting, auditing, and taxation services — meticulously tailored to secure and grow your business in an ever-evolving regulatory landscape.

Led by CA Poly John FCA, a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, we bring deep expertise and a personal commitment to every engagement — from individual ITR filings to large-scale corporate audits.
